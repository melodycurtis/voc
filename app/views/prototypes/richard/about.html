What is the service for?

Your pilot activity:

  • senior and management vacancies
  • teaching vacancies
  • apprentices

Your staff details:

  • personal details
  • job role and level
  • qualifications and experience
  • contract and salary

We need to know about your own staff, not subcontractors or other third party employees.



How can I get involved?

You may need to gather the data from these sources:

  • HR systems
  • MI systems
  • job specifications
  • job applications
  • CVs

The data collection is open for six weeks.
